ChronoAI Presents · Singapore's AI Builder Community
What's Next?

A monthly series built around the questions every builder asks after they ship — and nobody's created the right room to answer them. Until now.

The Series
Intimate. Curated. Built for builders who've shipped something and don't know what comes next.
Not a conference. Not a networking event dressed up as something useful. A monthly gathering of 50 builders, solopreneurs, and investors — each episode following a different phase of the solo founder journey.
50
Deliberately small. The right 50 people outperform a diluted room of 500.
Monthly
Recurring series. People build loyalty to a calendar, not a one-off event.
Free
Low barrier, high signal room. Quality curated, not gatekept by price.
Why ChronoAI Does This
Credibility through consistent presence — not through claims.
Every edition builds ChronoAI's position as the organising force behind Singapore's AI builder community. Credibility by association with Prof Liu Yang and practitioners like Hashmeta. Direct access to early-stage founders, solopreneurs, and investors shaping Singapore's AI landscape.
The Bigger Picture
What's Next becomes the foundation for an AI Collective.
Over time this series becomes the community platform bringing all ChronoAI's companies and partners together — creating commercial opportunity, talent pipelines, and long-term brand equity that no ad spend can replicate.
