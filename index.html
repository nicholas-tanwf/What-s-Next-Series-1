"What's
Next?"

A monthly series for Singapore's AI builder community — built around the questions that actually matter after you ship. Each episode follows a different phase of the solo founder journey, from your first app to your first revenue, your first expansion, and beyond.

一个为新加坡 AI 创造者社区打造的月度系列活动——围绕产品发布后真正重要的问题展开。每一期聚焦独立创业旅程的不同阶段，从第一款应用到第一笔收入、第一次扩张，直至更远的未来。

It's not a conference or a networking event dressed up as something useful. It's an intimate, curated gathering of builders, solopreneurs, and investors designed to generate honest conversations and real connections that keep people coming back. We start at 50 — deliberately small, deliberately curated — because the right 50 people will do more for ChronoAI's reputation than a diluted room of 500.

这不是一场会议，也不是一场伪装成有用活动的社交聚会。这是一个精心策划的小型聚会，汇集创造者、独立创业者和投资者，旨在产生真实的对话和有价值的连结。我们从 50 人开始——刻意保持小规模、精心筛选——因为对的 50 人比稀释的 500 人更有价值。

The Bigger Picture
更大的蓝图

Every edition of What's Next builds ChronoAI's position as the organising force behind Singapore's AI builder community — not through claims, but through consistent presence, credibility by association with speakers like Prof Liu Yang and industry practitioners like Hashmeta, and direct access to the exact audience we want to be known by: early-stage founders, solopreneurs, and investors who are shaping Singapore's AI landscape.

每一期"What's Next"都在巩固 ChronoAI 作为新加坡 AI 创造者社区核心推动力的地位——不是靠自我宣称，而是通过持续的在场、与刘杨教授等学术领袖和 Hashmeta 等行业实践者的信誉联结，直接触达我们最想被认识的受众：早期创始人、独立创业者和投资者。

End Goal: The AI Collective
终极目标：AI Collective

This series is the foundation for a broader AI Collective — a community platform that brings ChronoAI's network of companies and partners together in a way that creates commercial opportunity, talent pipelines, and long-term brand equity that no ad spend can replicate.

这个系列是更广阔的 AI Collective 的基石——一个社区平台，将 ChronoAI 的公司和合作伙伴网络汇聚在一起，创造商业机会、人才通道和长期品牌资产——这些是任何广告投入都无法复制的。
